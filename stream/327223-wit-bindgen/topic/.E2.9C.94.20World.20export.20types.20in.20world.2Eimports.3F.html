Topic: ✔ World export types in world.imports?

Alex Crichton (Nov 10 2025 at 16:25):

This is a decision that was made early on given the syntax of WIT where all types at the "root" of a world are considered imports, not exports. The main reason for that is a syntax limitation where the difference between imports and exports cannot be expressed in WIT right now

Alex Crichton (Nov 10 2025 at 16:26):

And then this wasn't ever fixed/improved on because top-level world types is a somewhat niche case compared to importing/exporting interfaces, so it's generally de-prioritized relative to everything else.

Alex Crichton (Nov 10 2025 at 16:26):

That being said IIRC there's an issue or two on the component-model repository tracking this and there was a plan at some point of how to improve it
