fitzgen (he/him) (Feb 26 2026 at 16:50):

@Scott Waye

What do you think will be the safety criticism with implementing these builtins and exploits? Is it limited to the implementers of the wasm runtime , wasmtime/browser or does it extended to source language to wasm compilers, e.g.clang?

the safety of compile-time builtins relies upon:

  • the wasm runtime (same as running any wasm at all)
  • the host-authored implementation of the builtins (they need e.g. bounds checks in front of unsafe loads, if necessary)
  • if the builtins are implemented in C/C++/Rust/etc, then you're also relying on the correctness of that language's compilation to wasm (e.g. it doesn't incorrectly optimize away your bounds checks)
