This PR adds several simplification rules:

bitops.isle

  • (~x & y) ^ x --> x | y
  • (x | y) & ~(x ^ y) --> x & y
  • x | ~(x ^ y) --> x | ~y
  • x | ((~x) ^ y) --> x | ~y
  • x & ~(x ^ y) --> x & y
  • x & ((~x) ^ y) --> x & y
  • (x | y) | (x ^ y) --> (x | y)
  • (x ^ y) & (x ^ (y ^ z)) --> (x ^ y) & ~z
  • (~x & y) ^ z --> (x & y) ^ (z ^ y)
  • ~x - ~y --> y - x
  • (~x & y) | ~(x | y) --> ~x
  • (~x | y) & ~(x & y) --> ~x
  • (x & y) | ~(x | y) --> ~(x ^ y)
  • (~x | y) ^ (x ^ y) --> x | ~y
