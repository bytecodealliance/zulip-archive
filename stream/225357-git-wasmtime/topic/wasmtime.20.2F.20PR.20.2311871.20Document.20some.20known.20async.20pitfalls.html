alexcrichton opened PR #11871 from alexcrichton:doc-some-pitfalls to bytecodealliance:main:

  • call_concurrent is more like a "spawn" operation within a store so it cannot be cancelled by dropping the returned future.
  • call_concurrent cannot be cancelled today other than by dropping the whole store. This is the subject of #11833.
  • call_concurrent cannot make progress outside of run_concurrent.
  • run_concurrent does not properly implement "select" operations internally due to #11869 and #11870. This means that timeouts within run_concurrent will not work.
