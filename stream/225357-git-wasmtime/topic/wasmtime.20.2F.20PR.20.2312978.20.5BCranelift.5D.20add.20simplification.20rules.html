arithmetic.isle

  • min/max(x,x) --> x
  • max(max(x, y), x) --> max(x, y)
  • min(min(x, y), x) --> min(x, y)
  • min(max(x, y), y) --> y ; max(min(x, y), y) --> y
  • min(min(x, y), max(x, y)) --> min(x,y)
  • max(min(x, y), max(x, y)) --> max(x, y)
  • x > max(x, y) --> 0
