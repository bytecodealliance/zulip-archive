Cranelift support exceptions, but it hasn't been wired up to Wasmtime yet. The RFC for adding it has been accepted: https://github.com/bytecodealliance/rfcs/pull/36 It is blocked on someone finding the time to actually implement it. Also cc https://github.com/bytecodealliance/wasmtime/issues/3427
